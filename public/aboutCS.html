About collab.soundscape

Instructions

collab.soundscape is a collaborative musical net art installation that enables users to create soundscapes and ambient music together using a web browser. To join the collective soundscape, simply enter your name and location on the home page. Once you have joined, you will be given a unique on-screen "firefly" to click and drag. The position of this firefly effects an audio sample that is assigned to you when you join. As you move your firefly around the screen, other fireflies (users) may begin to appear. At this point you will begin to hear other samples and changes created through their fireflies on-screen position. Listen to the resulting soundscape and contribute by placing your firefly in an ideal location. Feel free to experiment with your fireflies placement, hang out and chat with other users, or even plan a visit to the site with your friends for a group performance!
(If you do not hear any sound after joining, simply refresh the page and rejoin)

Technical Details

collab.soundscape uses the Nexus Hub and Tone.js frameworks to load and play back audio files and pass messages between users. In total there are 158 unique ambient audio samples that can be randomly assigned. The samples were gathered using the Freesound API with a filter of C minor.
